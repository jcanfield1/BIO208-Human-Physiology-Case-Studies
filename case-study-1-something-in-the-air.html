Phase 5 of 5  —  Final reflection

Resolution — 11:15 AM

The school has been evacuated. Emergency services confirmed elevated CO levels traced to an improperly reconnected flue pipe on the boiler after Friday's maintenance. Within 20 minutes outside, most students report their headaches fading and energy returning. No students required hospitalization. The nurse's early recognition and decisive action prevented a potentially life-threatening situation.
